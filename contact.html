Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn shoes. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact us for a prepaid return label.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. We also offer expedited 2-3 day shipping and overnight delivery options at checkout.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary from 10-21 business days depending on your location.

What's your warranty on shoes?

All Teke Shoes come with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. If you experience any issues, contact our support team.

How do I find my size?

Use our online size guide on the product page or contact us for personalized fit recommendations. We offer free returns if your size isn't right.

Are your shoes sustainable?

Yes! We use sustainable materials, ethical labor practices, and eco-friendly manufacturing processes. Learn more on our About page.
