Our Story

Building a legacy of quality and innovation since 2010

How It All Started

In 2010, founder Marcus Chen had a simple vision: create shoes that people would love to wear. Armed with a passion for craftsmanship and a background in sustainable design, he established Teke Shoes in a small workshop in Portland, Oregon.

What began as a modest operation has grown into a global brand trusted by hundreds of thousands of customers. Yet our core values remain unchangedâ€”quality over quantity, innovation without compromise, and a genuine commitment to our community.

Today, Teke Shoes represents more than just footwear. We're a movement toward sustainable fashion, ethical manufacturing, and the belief that your shoes should be as much a reflection of your values as they are of your style.

Our Mission

To create premium footwear that empowers individuals to express themselves while maintaining the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and ethical manufacturing.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted footwear brand, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction and social responsibility.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable materials, ethical labor practices, and responsible manufacturing processes in every step.

Community

We believe in giving back. A portion of every sale supports local communities, education initiatives, and charitable organizations making a real difference worldwide.
